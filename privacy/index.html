Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 26, 2026

V2M Player does not collect, store, transmit, or sell personal data. The app runs locally on your Mac and is used only to open and play .v2m music files that you explicitly select.

Data Collection

V2M Player does not collect:

  • Contact information
  • Usage analytics
  • Identifiers
  • Purchase history
  • Location data
  • Browsing history
  • User content uploaded to remote servers

File Access

The app only reads files that you explicitly open, drop into the app, or launch with the app from Finder. File access is used solely to play .v2m music files.

Network Access

V2M Player does not require network access for core playback.
